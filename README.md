# EloCalc

A small chess Elo calculator for iPhone and iPad.

## Privacy Policy

EloCalc does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us. The Elo calculator and any tournament data you enter stay on your device. The app has no accounts of its own, no analytics, no tracking, and no ads.

**Optional Lichess login.** You may optionally log in to Lichess to use the puzzle features. Logging in uses Lichess's official OAuth sign-in — your Lichess username and password are entered on Lichess and never touch this app. The app receives only an access token, which is kept securely in the device Keychain and used solely to talk to Lichess on your behalf (e.g. fetching your puzzle rating and reporting solved puzzles). Logging out removes the token from your device. When you are logged in, the app communicates with Lichess (lichess.org); their handling of your data is governed by Lichess's own privacy policy. If you never log in, the app makes no network connections.

If you contact support by email, your email address and message are used solely to respond to you and are not shared with anyone.
