# EloCalc

A tiny chess Elo calculator for iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

## Support

Questions, bug reports, or feedback? Email **geir.erik.nielsen@gmail.com**. I read every message and aim to reply within a few days.

## Privacy Policy

EloCalc does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All calculations and any tournament data you enter stay on your device. The app has no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no ads, and makes no network connections.

Because no data ever leaves your device, there is nothing for anyone to access, share, or sell. If you contact support by email, your email address and message are used solely to respond to you and are not shared with anyone.
